Vinicius Hernandes

I’m currently a PhD candidate in Physics at Delft University of Technology, where I’m part of the Quantum Matter and AI group, led by Eliska Greplova. My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and quantum physics.

Lately, I’ve been exploring how deep learning can be used to simulate quantum systems, speed up experiments, and develop new tools for scientific research. I’ve also created open-source software for analyzing neural data and simulating quantum dot devices.

Before starting my PhD, I completed a Master’s in Physics at the Federal University of Pelotas, where I focused on applying machine learning to soft matter systems.
